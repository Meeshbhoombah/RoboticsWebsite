WE ARE A FIRST ROBOTICS TEAM FROM SAINT JOSEPH HIGH SCHOOL LOCATED IN METUCHEN, NEW JERSEY.

The FIRST Robotics Competition is a outlet for high schoolers to express their ingenuity and originality. Since joining FIRST in 2005, Team 1626 has inspired over 500 students to do just that.

Our team doesn't just build robots. We build thinkers. Our students have a open mind and make measurable impact on the community.
